# Stakeholders

## Overview

ETA is an enterprise ecosystem that connects all participants involved in industrial procurement into one intelligent digital platform.

Each stakeholder contributes data, expertise, and business value while benefiting from automation, transparency, and AI-driven decision support.

# External Stakeholders

## Customers

Industrial organizations purchasing products and procurement services.

Needs:

- Reliability
- Transparency
- Speed
- Technical Expertise

---

## Manufacturers

Original equipment manufacturers supplying industrial products.

Needs:

- Qualified Customers
- Better Visibility
- Efficient Communication

---

## Suppliers

Authorized distributors and regional suppliers.

Needs:

- RFQ Access
- Long-Term Cooperation
- Digital Procurement Process

---

## Logistics Providers

Responsible for transportation, customs, and delivery.

Needs:

- Accurate Documentation
- Shipment Visibility
- Coordination

---

## Inspection Companies

Responsible for quality verification and compliance.

Needs:

- Technical Documentation
- Inspection Scheduling
- Traceability

---

## Technology Partners

Providers of AI, cloud infrastructure, integrations, and enterprise software.

Needs:

- Secure APIs
- Stable Architecture
- Long-Term Collaboration

---

# Stakeholder Objectives

ETA aligns all stakeholders around five common objectives:

- Transparency
- Efficiency
- Collaboration
- Knowledge Sharing
- Continuous Improvement

---

# Long-Term Vision

Every stakeholder becomes part of a continuously learning procurement ecosystem where knowledge is shared, processes are automated, and decisions are enhanced through artificial intelligence.
