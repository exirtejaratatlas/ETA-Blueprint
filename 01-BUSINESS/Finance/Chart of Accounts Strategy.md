# Chart of Accounts Strategy

# Purpose

This document defines the strategic architecture of the Enterprise Chart of Accounts (CoA) for the ETA Enterprise Business Operating System.

The objective is to establish a scalable, multi-dimensional accounting structure that supports:

- Iranian Accounting Standards
- Odoo ERP
- Management Accounting
- Project Accounting
- Procurement
- Sales
- AI Financial Analysis
- Tax Compliance
- Executive Reporting

The Chart of Accounts is designed specifically for a B2B trading company specializing in industrial procurement, oil & gas equipment, EPC procurement, steel trading, import and export.

---

# Design Principles

The Enterprise CoA shall be:

- Modular
- Scalable
- Multi-company Ready
- Multi-currency Ready
- AI-friendly
- IFRS-compatible
- Odoo-compatible
- Auditable
- Project-oriented
