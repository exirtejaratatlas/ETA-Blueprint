# Budget & Forecasting

# Purpose

This document defines the budgeting and forecasting framework for the ETA Enterprise Business Operating System.

The objective is to provide financial planning capabilities that support strategic decision-making, operational control and AI-driven forecasting across all business units.

---

# Budgeting Principles

ETA follows these principles:

- Budget Before Spending
- Rolling Forecast
- Scenario Planning
- Version Controlled Budgets
- Department Ownership
- Executive Approval
- Continuous Monitoring
