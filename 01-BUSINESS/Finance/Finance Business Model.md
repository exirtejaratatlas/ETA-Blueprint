# Finance Business Model

---

# Purpose

The Finance Business Model defines how financial value is created, controlled, measured and protected throughout the ETA Enterprise Business Operating System.

Unlike traditional accounting systems that only record transactions, ETA treats Finance as an operational intelligence domain that actively participates in every commercial process.

Every procurement activity, customer interaction, supplier engagement, contract, shipment and project ultimately generates financial events that become part of the enterprise financial model.
