# Management Accounting

# Purpose

This document defines the Enterprise Management Accounting framework for the ETA Enterprise Business Operating System.

Unlike Financial Accounting, which focuses on statutory reporting, Management Accounting is designed to support executive decision-making, operational performance analysis and AI-driven business optimization.

ETA operates in a project-driven B2B trading environment where profitability must be measured across customers, suppliers, contracts, projects, products and business units.

---

# Objectives

Management Accounting aims to:

- Measure business performance
- Improve profitability
- Support strategic planning
- Optimize procurement decisions
- Optimize pricing
- Support executive reporting
- Enable AI business analytics
- Improve operational efficiency
