# Accounting Processes

# Purpose

This document defines the accounting business processes governing all financial transactions within ETA Enterprise Business Operating System.

The accounting model is designed for an Iranian B2B trading company operating in:

- Industrial Equipment Trading
- Steel Trading
- Import
- Export
- EPC Procurement
- Project-based Contracts

The objective is to ensure every commercial activity automatically generates complete, auditable and compliant accounting records.

---

# Accounting Principles

ETA follows the following principles:

- Double Entry Accounting
- Accrual Accounting
- Auditability
- Traceability
- Compliance by Design
- Multi-Currency Accounting
- Project Accounting
- Cost Center Accounting
- Profit Center Accounting

# AI Support

AI assists accounting by:

- Detecting anomalies
- Suggesting account classifications
- Detecting duplicate invoices
- Predicting payment delays
- Explaining financial variances
- Forecasting cash flow

AI never posts accounting entries directly.

All postings require approval through enterprise governance.
