# Financial KPIs

# Purpose

This document defines the Enterprise Financial Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) used within ETA Enterprise Business Operating System.

The objective is to provide executives, finance teams and AI Agents with real-time visibility into the financial health, operational efficiency and profitability of Exir Tejarat Atlas.

Financial KPIs are grouped into strategic categories to support executive decision-making and continuous business improvement.
