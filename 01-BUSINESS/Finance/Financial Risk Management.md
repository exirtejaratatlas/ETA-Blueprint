# Financial Risk Management

# Purpose

This document defines the Enterprise Financial Risk Management Framework for the ETA Enterprise Business Operating System.

ETA operates as an international B2B procurement and trading organization exposed to financial, commercial, operational, contractual and regulatory risks.

Financial Risk Management enables continuous monitoring, early detection and AI-assisted mitigation of enterprise financial risks.

---

# Objectives

The framework aims to:

- Protect company assets
- Reduce financial losses
- Improve decision quality
- Detect financial anomalies
- Predict future risks
- Support executive governance
- Enable AI-assisted risk monitoring
- Maintain regulatory compliance

---

# Liquidity Risk

Liquidity Risk occurs when available cash is insufficient to meet operational obligations.

Monitored Items:

- Available Cash
- Upcoming Payments
- Payroll
- Taxes
- Supplier Payments
- Loan Repayments

AI predicts:

- Cash shortages
- Liquidity stress
- Emergency funding needs

---

# Credit Risk

Customer Credit Risk includes:

- Late Payments
- Default Probability
- Credit Limit Usage
- Collection History
- Outstanding Receivables

Each customer receives a dynamic Credit Risk Score.

---

# Supplier Risk

Supplier Risk evaluates:

- Financial Stability
- Delivery Reliability
- Compliance Status
- Country Risk
- Currency Exposure
- Dependency Level
- Price Volatility

The AI Supplier Agent continuously updates supplier risk profiles.

---

# Currency Risk

ETA monitors exposure across:

- IRR
- USD
- EUR
- AED
- CNY
- TRY

Risk indicators include:

- FX Volatility
- Open Positions
- Unrealized FX Loss
- Currency Concentration

# Banking Risk

Bank-related risks include:

- Bank Credit Rating
- Country Restrictions
- Transfer Delays
- Banking Sanctions
- Counterparty Exposure

# Business Rules

Example:

IF

Customer Risk Score > 85

THEN

Require CFO Approval Before New Sales Order

---

IF

Cash Forecast < 30 Days

THEN

Notify Treasury

AND

Freeze Non-Essential Spending

---

IF

Supplier Dependency > 40%

THEN

Recommend Alternative Suppliers

---

IF

Guarantee Expiry < 30 Days

THEN

Notify Legal Department

AND

Notify Project Manager
