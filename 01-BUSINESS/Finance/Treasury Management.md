# Cash Flow Management

# Purpose

This document defines the enterprise cash flow management framework for ETA Enterprise Business Operating System.

Cash Flow Management ensures that Exir Tejarat Atlas maintains sufficient liquidity to support procurement, project execution, supplier payments, customer operations, import/export activities and strategic growth.

The framework provides real-time visibility into cash movement while enabling AI-driven forecasting and financial planning.

---

# Objectives

The Cash Flow Management framework aims to:

- Maintain positive operational liquidity
- Optimize working capital
- Predict future cash shortages
- Improve payment planning
- Improve receivable collections
- Reduce idle cash
- Support executive financial planning
- Enable AI-assisted treasury decisions
