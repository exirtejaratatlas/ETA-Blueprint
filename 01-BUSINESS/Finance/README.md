# Finance, Accounting, Tax & Compliance Domain

## Overview

The Finance Domain is one of the Core Business Domains of the ETA Enterprise Business Operating System.

Unlike traditional ERP financial modules, this domain is designed as an enterprise financial intelligence platform that manages accounting, taxation, compliance, budgeting, profitability, cash flow, financial approvals and regulatory requirements across all procurement, sales, logistics and project operations.

The Finance Domain acts as the financial governance layer of ETA and provides trusted financial information for business users, executives, ERP systems and AI Agents.

---

Provide a complete financial operating model for Exir Tejarat Atlas that ensures:

- Financial transparency
- Regulatory compliance
- Accurate accounting
