# Target Market

## Primary Target Market

ETA is designed for medium-sized and large industrial organizations that manage complex procurement operations and require engineering-driven sourcing solutions.

---

# Primary Customer Segments

## Oil & Gas Companies

- Upstream Operators
- Midstream Companies
- Downstream Refineries
- Gas Processing Plants

---

## Petrochemical Companies

- Production Plants
- Maintenance Departments
- Procurement Departments
- Technical Engineering Teams

---

## Steel Industry

- Steel Mills
- Rolling Mills
- Pipe Manufacturers
- Foundries

---

## EPC Contractors

Engineering, Procurement and Construction companies responsible for large-scale industrial projects.

---

## Power Generation

- Thermal Power Plants
- Combined Cycle Plants
- Renewable Energy Projects

---

## Mining & Heavy Industry

Organizations requiring continuous procurement of industrial equipment and spare parts.

---

# Decision Makers

ETA primarily serves:

- Procurement Managers
- Supply Chain Directors
- Technical Managers
- Maintenance Managers
- Engineering Managers
- Project Managers
- Commercial Managers
- CEOs of Industrial Companies

# Secondary Market

- Industrial Manufacturers
- Authorized Distributors

---

# Customer Needs

Target customers require:

- Reliable global sourcing
- Faster procurement cycles
- Technical evaluation support
- Supplier qualification
- Procurement transparency
- AI-assisted decision making
- Digital document management
- Enterprise reporting

# Long-Term Market

ETA will evolve into a regional procurement ecosystem serving thousands of industrial organizations through a SaaS platform supported by artificial intelligence.
