# ETA Business Model

---

# Executive Summary

Exir Tejarat Atlas (ETA) is transforming from a traditional industrial trading company into an AI-powered Enterprise Procurement Ecosystem.

The company combines industrial procurement, engineering expertise, supplier intelligence, enterprise software, and artificial intelligence into one integrated business model.

ETA operates simultaneously as:

- Industrial Procurement Company
- Engineering Procurement Partner
- Enterprise Software Provider
- Procurement Intelligence Platform
- AI-enabled Knowledge Company

The long-term objective is to become the operating system of industrial procurement across the Middle East.

---

# Business Vision

ETA does not compete merely by selling products.

ETA competes by reducing procurement cost, procurement time, procurement risk, and procurement complexity through intelligent technology.
