# Business Model

## Business Overview

Exir Tejarat Atlas (ETA) is an industrial procurement and technology company that combines international trading, engineering expertise, supplier intelligence, and artificial intelligence into one enterprise ecosystem.

The business operates through two complementary models:

1. Industrial Procurement & Trading
2. Enterprise SaaS Platform (ETA Platform)
