# Brand Voice

## Personality

ETA communicates as an experienced industrial engineering partner rather than a software vendor or a trading company.

Our personality is:

- Professional
- Intelligent
- Precise
- Honest
- Modern
- Confident
- Calm
- Trustworthy

---

# Tone of Voice

ETA always communicates with:

- Technical accuracy
- Business clarity
- Respect
- Transparency
- Confidence
- Simplicity

---

# We Are

- Engineering Driven
- AI Native
- Enterprise Focused
- Knowledge Centric
- Customer Oriented
- Innovation Focused

---

# We Are Not

- Overly Marketing-Oriented
- Loud
- Aggressive
- Buzzword Driven
- Overly Casual
- Sales Focused

---

# Writing Principles

Always:

- Explain clearly
- Keep language concise
- Support statements with facts
- Focus on business value
- Maintain technical credibility

Never:

- Exaggerate
- Make unsupported claims
- Use unnecessary buzzwords
- Overcomplicate language
