# Value Proposition

## What Makes ETA Different

ETA is the first AI-native industrial procurement ecosystem that combines engineering expertise, global sourcing, supplier intelligence, enterprise software, and artificial intelligence into one integrated platform.

Unlike traditional trading companies, ETA does not simply supply products.

Unlike conventional ERP systems, ETA does not only manage data.

ETA actively assists organizations in making better procurement decisions.

# Customer Benefits

ETA enables organizations to:

- Reduce procurement cycle time
- Improve supplier selection
- Increase procurement transparency
- Preserve engineering knowledge
- Centralize procurement information
- Automate repetitive processes
- Improve commercial negotiations
- Reduce procurement risk
- Increase operational efficiency
