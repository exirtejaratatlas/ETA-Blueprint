# ETA Enterprise Email Templates

## Purpose

This document standardizes all external and internal email communications to ensure consistency, professionalism, and alignment with the ETA brand.

Every email should be concise, respectful, technically accurate, and focused on creating value for the recipient.

---

# General Principles

Every email should:

- Use a professional tone
- Be easy to read
- Focus on the recipient
- Clearly state the purpose
- End with a clear next action
- Reflect ETA's engineering-first culture

# Initial Customer Introduction

Subject:

Introduction to Exir Tejarat Atlas

Purpose:

Introduce ETA and request an introductory meeting.

Structure:

- Greeting
- Company introduction
- Core capabilities
- Industries served
- Request for a meeting
- Contact information

---

# Follow-Up After Meeting

Subject:

Thank You for Your Time

Purpose:

Summarize the meeting and define next steps.

Include:

- Appreciation
- Meeting summary
- Agreed actions
- Timeline
- Contact information

---

# RFQ Submission

Subject:

Quotation Submission – [Project / RFQ Number]

Structure:

- Reference number
- Attached quotation
- Commercial validity
- Delivery schedule
- Technical documents
- Contact person

---

# Proposal Submission

Subject:

Commercial & Technical Proposal

Purpose:

Deliver a complete proposal package.

Attachments may include:

- Proposal
- Technical Compliance
- Company Profile
- Datasheets
- Certifications

---

# Supplier Introduction

Subject:

Supplier Partnership Opportunity

Purpose:

Invite manufacturers or suppliers to collaborate with ETA.

Topics:

- Company overview
- Procurement ecosystem
- Partnership benefits
- Registration process

---

# Customer Follow-Up

Subject:

Following Up Regarding Your Inquiry

Purpose:

Maintain communication without being overly promotional.

Include:

- Previous discussion reference
- Assistance offered
- Request for updates
- Next action

---

# Project Status Update

Subject:

Project Status Update

Include:

- Current progress
- Procurement status
- Delivery schedule
- Risks (if any)
- Next milestone

---

# Event Invitation

Subject:

Invitation to Meet ETA

Purpose:

Invite customers or partners to:

- Exhibition
- Webinar
- Technical Workshop
- Business Meeting

---

# Newsletter

Frequency:

Monthly

Sections:

- Industry Insights
- ETA Updates
- AI Procurement
- Engineering Articles
- Customer Stories
- Upcoming Events

---

# Tone of Voice

ETA emails should always feel:

- Professional
- Engineering Driven
- Helpful
- Respectful
- Knowledgeable
- Trustworthy

Never use:

- Aggressive sales language
- Misleading claims
- Excessive marketing buzzwords
- Informal expressions

---

# Long-Term Goal

Every email sent by ETA should strengthen customer trust, reinforce the company's expertise, and contribute to long-term business relationships rather than short-term sales.
