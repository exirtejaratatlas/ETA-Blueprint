# ETA Sales Materials Framework

## Purpose

Sales materials support enterprise sales conversations by presenting ETA's capabilities in a clear, professional, and value-driven manner.

The objective is not to sell products—it is to build confidence in ETA as a long-term procurement and technology partner.

---

# Sales Toolkit

Every sales representative should have access to:

- Company Profile
- Corporate Presentation
- Capability Statement
- Product & Service One-Pagers
- Case Studies
- Customer References
- Technical Datasheets
- Proposal Templates
- RFQ Response Templates
- Supplier Introduction Pack

---

# Core Documents

## Company Profile

High-level introduction to ETA, its mission, industries, and capabilities.

---

## Corporate Presentation

Executive presentation for meetings with customers, partners, and investors.

---

## Capability Statement

A concise overview highlighting:

- Core Competencies
- Industries Served
- Competitive Advantages
- Key Services

---

## Service One-Pagers

Dedicated documents for:

- Industrial Procurement
- Engineering Support
- Global Sourcing
- AI Procurement Platform
- Supplier Intelligence

---

## Case Studies

Each case study should include:

- Customer Challenge
- ETA Solution
- Implementation
- Results
- Lessons Learned

---

## Proposal Templates

Standard proposal structure:

1. Executive Summary
2. Customer Requirements
3. Proposed Solution
4. Scope of Supply
5. Technical Compliance
6. Commercial Offer
7. Delivery Schedule
8. Company Credentials
9. Terms & Conditions

---

# Presentation Standards

Every sales document should be:

- Professional
- Clean
- Minimal
- Brand Consistent
- Engineering Focused
- Easy to Read

---

# Messaging Principles

Focus on:

- Customer Outcomes
- Business Value
- Engineering Expertise
- Risk Reduction
- Procurement Efficiency
- Long-Term Partnership

Avoid:

- Aggressive sales language
- Unsupported claims
- Excessive marketing buzzwords

---

# Target Audiences

Sales materials should be adaptable for:

- CEOs
- Procurement Managers
- Supply Chain Directors
- Engineering Managers
- EPC Contractors
- Manufacturers
- Investors

---

# Success Metrics

Evaluate sales materials by:

- Proposal Win Rate
- Meeting Conversion Rate
- RFQ Conversion
- Customer Feedback
- Sales Cycle Duration

---

# Long-Term Goal

Create a standardized, enterprise-grade sales toolkit that enables every ETA representative to communicate a consistent message, shorten the sales cycle, and strengthen customer trust.
