# ETA Marketing Documentation

## Overview

This directory contains the complete marketing framework for the ETA Enterprise Ecosystem.

The documentation defines how ETA communicates with the market, generates demand, supports sales, builds trust, and positions itself as the leading AI-native enterprise procurement platform.

Marketing is treated as a strategic business capability rather than a collection of campaigns.

---

# Structure

## Campaigns

Marketing campaigns, launch plans, seasonal initiatives, and enterprise growth programs.

---

## Sales Materials

Sales collateral including presentations, one-pagers, capability statements, proposal templates, and customer-facing documents.

---

## Social Media

Communication strategy for LinkedIn and other professional channels, including content pillars, posting guidelines, and engagement standards.

---

## Email Templates

Standardized email templates for sales, partnerships, customer onboarding, follow-ups, supplier communication, and marketing automation.

---

# Objectives

The ETA marketing system exists to:

- Build brand awareness
- Generate qualified enterprise leads
- Support the sales process
- Educate the market
- Demonstrate engineering expertise
- Position ETA as an AI-native procurement leader
