# ETA Marketing Campaign Framework

## Purpose

Marketing campaigns are designed to educate the industrial market, build trust, generate qualified enterprise leads, and position ETA as the leading AI-native procurement ecosystem.

Campaigns should always focus on solving business problems—not selling products.

---

# Campaign Objectives

- Increase Brand Awareness
- Generate Qualified Leads
- Build Market Authority
- Educate Industrial Decision Makers
- Support Enterprise Sales
- Strengthen Customer Relationships

---

# Campaign Categories

## Brand Campaigns

Purpose:

Increase recognition of ETA as an innovative engineering and procurement company.

Examples:

- Who is ETA
- Why ETA
- Engineering Excellence
- AI for Procurement

---

## Product Campaigns

Focus:

Promote ETA Platform capabilities.

Examples:

- AI Procurement Assistant
- Supplier Portal
- Manufacturer Portal
- Procurement Dashboard
- Enterprise Knowledge Base

---

## Industry Campaigns

Focus on specific sectors.

Examples:

- Steel Industry
- Oil & Gas
- Petrochemical
- Mining
- Power Generation
- EPC Companies

---

## Educational Campaigns

Provide practical value.

Topics include:

- Procurement Best Practices
- Supplier Evaluation
- Procurement Digital Transformation
- Engineering Procurement
- AI Adoption
- Industrial Sourcing

---

## Customer Success Campaigns

Highlight:

- Case Studies
- Success Stories
- Project Results
- Customer Testimonials

---

## Partnership Campaigns

Promote collaboration with:

- Manufacturers
- Suppliers
- Technology Partners
- Engineering Firms

---

# Campaign Channels

Primary:

- Website
- LinkedIn
- Email Marketing

Secondary:

- Industry Events
- Conferences
- Webinars
- Technical Workshops
- Business Meetings

# Campaign Lifecycle

1. Research

2. Planning

3. Content Creation

4. Design

5. Launch

6. Distribution

7. Lead Capture

8. Performance Analysis

9. Optimization

---

# KPIs

- Website Traffic
- Qualified Leads
- Sales Meetings
- Proposal Requests
- RFQs Received
- Conversion Rate
- Email Open Rate
- LinkedIn Engagement
- Customer Acquisition Cost
- Marketing ROI

---

# Campaign Principles

Every campaign should:

- Teach before selling
- Demonstrate expertise
- Solve real problems
- Build long-term trust
- Support enterprise decision makers
- Reinforce the ETA brand

---

# Long-Term Goal

Develop a marketing engine that continuously attracts, educates, and converts industrial organizations into long-term ETA partners while establishing ETA as the most trusted procurement intelligence brand in the region.
