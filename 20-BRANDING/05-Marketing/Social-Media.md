# ETA Social Media Strategy

## Purpose

ETA uses social media to educate the industrial market, build credibility, strengthen its brand, and establish thought leadership in engineering, procurement, and artificial intelligence.

The objective is not to generate viral content but to become the most trusted voice in enterprise procurement.

---

# Primary Platform

## LinkedIn

LinkedIn is ETA's primary communication channel.

Target audience:

- CEOs
- Procurement Managers
- Supply Chain Directors
- Engineering Managers
- EPC Companies
- Manufacturers
- Suppliers
- Investors

# Tone of Voice

Every post should be:

- Professional
- Educational
- Clear
- Honest
- Technical
- Helpful

Avoid:

- Clickbait
- Sensational headlines
- Political topics
- Controversial discussions
- Excessive self-promotion

---
