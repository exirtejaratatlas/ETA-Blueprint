# Exir Tejarat Atlas (ETA)

## Company Overview

Exir Tejarat Atlas (ETA) is an industrial procurement, engineering, and technology company specializing in supplying equipment, materials, and intelligent procurement solutions for heavy industries.

Founded with the vision of transforming industrial procurement, ETA combines engineering expertise, international sourcing, supplier intelligence, and artificial intelligence into a single enterprise ecosystem.

Today, ETA serves organizations operating in the steel, oil & gas, petrochemical, power generation, mining, cement, and heavy manufacturing sectors.
