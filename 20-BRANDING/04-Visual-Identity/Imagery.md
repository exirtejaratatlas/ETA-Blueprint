# ETA Imagery System

## Philosophy

Images are used to communicate capability, trust, engineering excellence, and industrial intelligence.

Every visual should reinforce ETA's identity as an AI-native enterprise procurement company.

---

# Photography Style

Photography should be:

- Authentic
- Industrial
- Premium
- Minimal
- Clean
- Modern

Preferred subjects:

- Steel production
- Manufacturing facilities
- Oil & Gas plants
- Petrochemical complexes
- Engineering teams
- Industrial inspections
- Procurement operations
- Logistics and shipping
- Modern offices
- Digital technology

Avoid:

- Generic stock photos
- Artificial corporate smiles
- Over-saturated colors
- Low-quality imagery
- Busy compositions

---

# Illustration Style

Illustrations should be:

- Flat
- Geometric
- Minimal
- Technical
- Enterprise-focused

Use illustrations to explain:

- Procurement workflows
- AI processes
- Supplier ecosystems
- Platform architecture
- Dashboards
- Enterprise systems

---

# AI Generated Images

AI-generated visuals must maintain:

- Realistic lighting
- Engineering accuracy
- Industrial credibility
- Consistent color palette
- Minimal visual noise

Never generate unrealistic machinery or technically impossible industrial environments.

---

# Image Color Treatment

Preferred tone:

- Neutral
- Cool
- Steel-inspired
- Natural lighting

Copper accents may be used only to highlight key objects.

---

# Composition

Preferred compositions:

- Wide industrial environments
- Symmetry
- Leading lines
- Large negative space
- Clear focal points

---

# People

When people appear:

- Engineers
- Procurement specialists
- Plant operators
- Technical inspectors
- Business professionals

Diversity and professionalism should be represented naturally.

---

# Backgrounds

Preferred:

- Industrial facilities
- Manufacturing lines
- Technical drawings
- Digital interfaces
- Clean architectural environments

---

# Motion Graphics

Motion should communicate:

- Process
- Data Flow
- AI Intelligence
- Procurement Journey
- Enterprise Connectivity

Animation must remain subtle and purposeful.

---

# Overall Impression

ETA imagery should feel:

- Intelligent
- Industrial
- Premium
- Enterprise
- Innovative
- Trustworthy

Every visual should strengthen confidence in the ETA ecosystem.
