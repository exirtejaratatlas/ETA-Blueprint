# ETA Enterprise Workflow

## Purpose

This document defines the end-to-end operational workflow of the ETA Enterprise Ecosystem.

It describes how information, decisions, users, and AI collaborate throughout the industrial procurement lifecycle.

The workflow is designed to maximize transparency, efficiency, traceability, and intelligent decision-making.

# AI Responsibilities

AI participates throughout the workflow by providing:

- Company Research
- Customer Intelligence
- Supplier Recommendation
- Technical Document Analysis
- Datasheet Comparison
- Commercial Recommendation
- Delivery Prediction
- Knowledge Search
- Executive Insights
- Workflow Automation

AI supports decision-making but does not replace human approval for critical business decisions.

# Workflow Principles

Every workflow should be:

- Traceable
- Auditable
- AI Assisted
- Role Based
- Secure
- Automated where appropriate
- Customer Focused
- Engineering Driven

# Long-Term Vision

The ETA workflow becomes a continuously learning procurement engine where every completed project improves future decisions through accumulated enterprise knowledge and AI-driven intelligence.
