# Compliance Domain

## Purpose

The Compliance Domain ensures that every business process within the ETA Enterprise Ecosystem complies with applicable laws, regulations, contractual obligations, corporate governance policies, and internal procedures.

It provides enterprise-wide governance, auditability, traceability, and risk management across all operational domains.

---

# Business Objectives

The Compliance Domain enables ETA to:

- Ensure regulatory compliance.
- Enforce corporate policies.
- Monitor contractual obligations.
- Support internal and external audits.
- Reduce operational risk.
- Maintain full traceability.
- Protect organizational integrity.
- Support international business standards.
