# Customer Domain

## Purpose

The Customer Domain manages the complete lifecycle of industrial customers within the ETA Enterprise Ecosystem.

It centralizes customer master data, commercial relationships, project history, procurement activities, financial interactions, and AI-driven customer intelligence.

The Customer Domain acts as the single source of truth for every customer interacting with ETA.

---

# Business Objectives

The Customer Domain enables ETA to:

- Maintain accurate customer master data.
- Build long-term business relationships.
- Support sales and procurement collaboration.
- Improve customer satisfaction.
- Preserve customer knowledge.
- Reduce duplicated information.
- Provide AI-powered customer insights.
- Support executive decision making.

# Business Rules

- Every customer has a unique Customer ID.
- Duplicate legal entities are not permitted.
- Credit limits are managed by Finance.
- Customer status controls business operations.
- All customer changes are fully audited.
