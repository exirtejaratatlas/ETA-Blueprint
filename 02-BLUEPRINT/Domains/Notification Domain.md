# Notification Domain

## Purpose

The Notification Domain manages all enterprise communications generated by the ETA Ecosystem.

It delivers timely, secure, and context-aware notifications to employees, customers, suppliers, manufacturers, AI agents, and external systems through multiple communication channels.

The goal is to ensure that every important business event reaches the right stakeholder at the right time.

---

# Business Objectives

The Notification Domain enables ETA to:

- Deliver real-time business notifications.
- Reduce communication delays.
- Improve operational awareness.
- Automate event-driven messaging.
- Support multi-channel communication.
- Improve workflow efficiency.
- Provide complete notification auditability.
