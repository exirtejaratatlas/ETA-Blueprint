# Supplier Domain

## Purpose

The Supplier Domain manages the complete lifecycle of suppliers within the ETA Enterprise Ecosystem.

It centralizes supplier master data, qualifications, certifications, commercial history, technical capabilities, performance metrics, and compliance information to support intelligent procurement decisions.

The Supplier Domain is one of ETA's strategic knowledge assets.

---

# Business Objectives

The Supplier Domain enables ETA to:

- Build a trusted supplier network.
- Reduce supplier risk.
- Improve supplier quality.
- Preserve supplier knowledge.
- Support AI-driven supplier recommendations.
- Standardize vendor qualification.
- Continuously evaluate supplier performance.

# Business Rules

- Every Supplier has one unique Supplier Code.
- Legal registration is mandatory.
- Certifications have expiration dates.
- Supplier ratings are recalculated after every completed procurement.
- Suspended suppliers cannot receive RFQs.
- Blacklisted suppliers cannot participate in procurement.
