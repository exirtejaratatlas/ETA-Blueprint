# AI Domain

## Purpose

The AI Domain is the intelligence layer of the ETA Enterprise Ecosystem.

Rather than functioning as a chatbot, the AI Domain operates as a collection of enterprise agents, reasoning engines, retrieval systems, orchestration services, and decision-support components that enhance every business process.

Artificial Intelligence is a core enterprise capability embedded across CRM, Procurement, Finance, Inventory, Projects, Supplier Management, and Executive Decision Making.

---

# Business Objectives

The AI Domain enables ETA to:

- Assist every department.
- Preserve organizational knowledge.
- Improve procurement decisions.
- Reduce repetitive work.
- Accelerate engineering analysis.
- Support executives with insights.
- Automate enterprise workflows.
- Learn continuously from enterprise data.
