# ETA Product Universe

## Vision

ETA is an AI-native Enterprise Procurement Ecosystem that unifies industrial procurement, engineering knowledge, supplier collaboration, customer relationship management, and intelligent decision support within a single platform.

Rather than being a standalone CRM or ERP, ETA serves as the operating system for industrial procurement organizations.

---

# Product Philosophy

ETA is built on five principles:

- Engineering First
- AI Native
- Enterprise Grade
- Modular by Design
- Knowledge Driven

---

# Product Ecosystem

The ETA ecosystem consists of interconnected products that share a common data model, identity system, and AI layer.

---

# Core Platforms

## Executive Workspace

Strategic dashboards and executive decision support.

---

## CRM

Customer lifecycle management.

Capabilities include:

- Accounts
- Contacts
- Opportunities
- Quotations
- Activities
- Meetings
- Pipeline
- Sales Analytics

---

## Procurement Platform

The operational core of ETA.

Capabilities include:

- RFQ Management
- Supplier Selection
- Technical Evaluation
- Commercial Comparison
- Purchase Orders
- Delivery Tracking
- Procurement Analytics

---

## Supplier Portal

Workspace for suppliers to:

- Receive RFQs
- Submit quotations
- Upload documents
- Track performance
- Communicate with ETA

---

## Manufacturer Portal

Workspace for manufacturers to:

- Publish product information
- Maintain catalogs
- Upload certifications
- Respond to technical requests
- Manage partnerships

---

## AI Workspace

Central intelligence layer providing:

- AI Procurement Assistant
- Enterprise Search
- Technical Q&A
- Document Intelligence
- Recommendation Engine
- Knowledge Retrieval
- Workflow Automation

---

## Knowledge Base

Enterprise repository containing:

- Datasheets
- Standards
- Specifications
- Vendor Information
- Historical Projects
- Technical Documents
- Lessons Learned

---

## Analytics Platform

Enterprise reporting including:

- Executive KPIs
- Procurement Performance
- Sales Performance
- Supplier Performance
- Financial Indicators
- AI Usage Metrics
