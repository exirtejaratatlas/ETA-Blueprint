# Product Entity

## Purpose

The Product entity represents every commercial, industrial, engineering, procurement, inventory, and service item managed throughout the ETA Enterprise Ecosystem.

Products form the core of procurement, sales, inventory, engineering, logistics, AI, and ERP processes.
