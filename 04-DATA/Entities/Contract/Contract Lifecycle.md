# Contract Lifecycle

## Purpose

The Contract Lifecycle defines the complete enterprise journey of a contract from initial drafting through approval, execution, amendments, renewal, expiration, and archival.

The lifecycle ensures legal governance, commercial transparency, procurement control, financial integrity, compliance monitoring, and AI-assisted contract intelligence.

---

Draft

Internal Review

Legal Review

↓

# Stage 1 — Draft

Contract is initially prepared.

Activities

- Define parties
- Define scope
- Draft clauses
- Commercial terms
- Technical annexes

Status

```
Draft
```

---

# Stage 2 — Internal Review

Business stakeholders review the draft.

Participants

- Procurement
- Sales
- Engineering
- Finance

Status

```
Internal Review
```

---

# Stage 3 — Legal Review

Legal department validates:

- Clauses
- Risks
- Jurisdiction
- Compliance
- Obligations

Status

```
Legal Review
```

---

# Stage 4 — Commercial Review

Commercial team validates:

- Pricing
- Payment Terms
- Incoterms
- Warranty
- Commercial commitments

Status

```
Commercial Review
```

---

# Stage 5 — Approval

Contract enters approval workflow.

Possible approvers

- Legal
- Procurement
- Finance
- Executive

Status

```
Pending Approval
```

---

# Stage 6 — Signed

Both parties sign the contract.

Activities

- Digital signature
- Physical signature
- Document registration

Status

```
Signed
```

---

# Stage 7 — Active

Contract becomes legally effective.

The contract may now govern:

- RFQs
- Quotations
- Purchase Orders
- Deliveries
- Invoices
- Payments

Status

```
Active
```

---

# Stage 8 — Execution

Business obligations are executed.

Examples

- Product delivery
- Services
- Support
- Warranty
- SLA monitoring

Status

```
Execution
```

---

# Stage 9 — Amendment

Contract modifications may include:

- Price changes
- Scope changes
- Duration changes
- Technical revisions

Each amendment creates a new version while preserving previous history.

Status

```
Amended
```

---

# Stage 10 — Renewal

Eligible contracts may be renewed.

Activities

- Renewal review
- AI renewal prediction
- Commercial negotiation
- Executive approval

Status

```
Renewed
```

---

# Stage 11 — Expiration

Contract validity period ends.

Possible outcomes

- Renew
- Close
- Archive

Status

```
Expired
```

---

# Stage 12 — Closure

All contractual obligations have been fulfilled.

Examples

- Final payment
- Warranty completion
- Claims resolved

Status

```
Closed
```

---

# Stage 13 — Archived

Historical contracts remain available for:

- Audit
- Legal reference
- Supplier evaluation
- AI learning
- Analytics

Status

```
Archived
```
