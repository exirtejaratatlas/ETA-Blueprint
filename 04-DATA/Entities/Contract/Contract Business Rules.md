# Contract Business Rules

## Purpose

This document defines the enterprise business rules governing contracts throughout the ETA Enterprise Ecosystem.

These rules ensure legal compliance, procurement governance, financial integrity, supplier accountability, contract lifecycle control, and complete auditability.

---

# Identity Rules

## BR-001

Every Contract shall have one globally unique Contract ID.

---

## BR-002

Contract Number must be unique.

---

## BR-003

Contract Type is mandatory.

---

## BR-004

Every Contract must have at least two legal parties.

---

## BR-005

Only one active version of the same contract may exist.

---

# Approval Rules

## BR-006

Contracts require Legal approval before becoming Active.

---

## BR-007

Commercial approval is mandatory.

---

## BR-008

Financial approval is required above approval thresholds.

---

## BR-009

Executive approval is required for strategic contracts.

---

# Validity Rules

## BR-010

Effective Date cannot be later than Expiration Date.

---

## BR-011

Expired contracts automatically become inactive unless renewed.

---

## BR-012

Renewals create a new contract version while preserving history.

---

# Commercial Rules

## BR-013

Currency is mandatory.

---

## BR-014

Payment Terms are mandatory.

---

## BR-015

Incoterm is mandatory where applicable.

---

## BR-016

Warranty obligations must be defined when applicable.

---

## BR-017

Penalty clauses must be documented.

---

# Procurement Rules

## BR-018

Purchase Orders may only reference Active contracts.

---

## BR-019

Contract value limits must not be exceeded without amendment.

---

## BR-020

Framework agreements may govern multiple Purchase Orders.

---

# Compliance Rules

## BR-021

Compliance requirements shall be documented.

---

## BR-022

Non-compliance events shall be recorded.

---

## BR-023

Every amendment requires legal approval.

---

## BR-024

Every signed contract shall be stored in Document Management.

---

# AI Rules

## BR-025

AI recommendations are advisory only.

---

## BR-026

AI may identify risky clauses.

---

## BR-027

AI may predict contract renewal probability.

---

## BR-028

AI shall never modify contractual content automatically.

---

# Audit Rules

## BR-029

Every contract modification shall be audited.

---

## BR-030

Contracts use Soft Delete only.

---

## BR-031

Signed contracts become read-only except through formal amendments.

---

## BR-032

All approvals shall be permanently traceable.

---

# Enterprise Rule Summary

The Contract governance framework guarantees:

- Legal integrity
- Commercial governance
- Procurement control
- Financial compliance
- Lifecycle management
- AI governance
- Complete enterprise auditability

---

# Long-Term Vision

The Contract Business Rules establish the legal governance framework of ETA, ensuring every commercial relationship is executed through controlled approvals, compliance monitoring, procurement integration, AI-assisted risk analysis, and complete lifecycle traceability.
