# Contract Entity

## Purpose

The Contract entity represents the legally binding agreement between ETA and a supplier, customer, manufacturer, or business partner.

It governs commercial obligations, technical commitments, financial terms, legal clauses, warranties, service levels, and long-term procurement relationships.

Contracts serve as the legal foundation for Purchase Orders, Deliveries, Payments, and Claims.
