# Purchase Order Lifecycle

## Purpose

The Purchase Order Lifecycle defines the complete operational journey of a Purchase Order from creation until procurement closure and archival.

It ensures controlled procurement execution, supplier commitment, logistics coordination, inventory synchronization, financial processing, and enterprise governance.

# Stage 1 — Draft

Procurement prepares the Purchase Order.

Activities

- Product selection
- Pricing
- Commercial terms
- Delivery schedule
- Internal review

