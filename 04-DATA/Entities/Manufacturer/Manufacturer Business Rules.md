# Manufacturer Business Rules

## Purpose

This document defines the enterprise business rules governing Manufacturer records across the ETA Enterprise Ecosystem.

These rules ensure engineering integrity, procurement governance, OEM traceability, compliance, and AI readiness.

---

# Identity Rules

## BR-001

Every Manufacturer must have one unique Manufacturer ID.

---

## BR-002

Manufacturer Code must be unique across the enterprise.

---

## BR-003

Duplicate Legal Company Names are prohibited.

---

## BR-004

A Manufacturer cannot be both Active and Archived.

---

# Registration Rules

## BR-005

Manufacturer Type is mandatory.

---

## BR-006

Country is mandatory.

---

## BR-007

Company Name is mandatory.

---

## BR-008

Registration Number is mandatory for corporate manufacturers.

---

## BR-009

Primary Manufacturing Location must be defined.

---

# Engineering Rules

## BR-010

Every Manufacturer must support at least one Product Category.

---

## BR-011

Engineering Capabilities must be documented before Approval.

---

## BR-012

Production Capacity must be maintained for strategic manufacturers.

---

## BR-013

Manufacturer standards must be recorded.

---

## BR-014

Technical documentation must be linked to the Manufacturer profile.

---

# Compliance Rules

## BR-015

KYC approval is required before activation.

---

## BR-016

AML screening must pass before activation.

---

## BR-017

Sanctioned manufacturers cannot become Active.

---

## BR-018

Expired certifications generate Compliance alerts.

---

## BR-019

Compliance reviews must occur periodically.

---

# Product Rules

## BR-020

Every Product must reference one Primary Manufacturer.

---

## BR-021

Manufacturer changes require Product review.

---

## BR-022

Obsolete Manufacturers cannot receive new Product assignments.

---

# Procurement Rules

## BR-023

Only Approved Manufacturers may participate in sourcing.

---

## BR-024

Preferred Manufacturers receive sourcing priority.

---

## BR-025

Strategic Manufacturers require Executive approval.

---

# AI Rules

## BR-026

Every Active Manufacturer must maintain an AI Summary.

---

## BR-027

AI Capability Scores are advisory only.

---

## BR-028

AI Risk Scores never override Compliance decisions.

---

# Audit Rules

## BR-029

Every Manufacturer modification is audited.

---

## BR-030

Manufacturer deletion is prohibited.

Only Soft Delete (Archive) is allowed.

---

# Enterprise Rule Summary

Critical enterprise rules include:

- Unique Manufacturer Code
- Mandatory Engineering Validation
- Mandatory Compliance
- Product Traceability
- OEM Governance
- Complete Audit Trail

---

# Long-Term Vision

Business rules ensure that Manufacturer data remains trusted, technically accurate, compliant, AI-ready, and fully aligned with enterprise engineering and procurement governance across ETA.
