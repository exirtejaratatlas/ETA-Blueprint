# Inventory Lifecycle

## Purpose

The Inventory Lifecycle defines the complete operational journey of inventory from receiving through storage, reservation, warehouse operations, consumption, replenishment, valuation, and archival.

The lifecycle guarantees inventory accuracy, procurement synchronization, warehouse governance, financial consistency, and AI-driven inventory optimization.
