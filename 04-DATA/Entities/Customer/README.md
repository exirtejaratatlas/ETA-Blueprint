# Customer Entity

## Purpose

The Customer entity represents every organization, company, government body, contractor, EPC company, industrial plant, distributor, or business partner that purchases products or services from Exir Tejarat Atlas.

The Customer entity is the commercial foundation of the ETA Enterprise Ecosystem and serves as the master record for all customer-related activities.
