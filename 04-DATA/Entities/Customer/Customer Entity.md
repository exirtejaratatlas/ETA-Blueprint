# Customer Entity

## Purpose

The Customer entity represents every organization that purchases products, engineering services, procurement services, consulting services, or digital platform subscriptions from Exir Tejarat Atlas.

Customer is a Master Data entity and acts as the commercial identity of every buyer across the ETA Enterprise Ecosystem.

This entity is shared across CRM, Sales, Procurement, Contracts, Projects, Finance, AI, Reporting, and Customer Portal.

---

A Customer may be:

- EPC Contractor
- Government Organization
- Oil & Gas Company
- Petrochemical Company
- Steel Manufacturer
- Mining Company
- Cement Company
- Power Plant

- Trading Company

Every customer has one enterprise profile regardless of how many projects or contracts exist.
