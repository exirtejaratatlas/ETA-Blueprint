# Customer Lifecycle

## Purpose

The Customer Lifecycle defines the complete evolution of a customer throughout the ETA Enterprise Ecosystem.

It standardizes every stage from the first contact through long-term partnership while ensuring governance, automation, and AI-driven optimization.

# Stage Definitions

## Lead

A potential organization identified through marketing, referrals, tenders, exhibitions, or direct outreach.

Characteristics

- Minimal information
- No commercial engagement
- Not yet qualified

---

## Qualified Lead

Basic qualification completed.

Requirements

- Industry identified
- Contact verified
- Potential business confirmed

---

## Prospect

A company considered suitable for future cooperation.

Typical Activities

- Initial meetings
- Capability presentation
- Product discussions

---

## Opportunity

A real commercial opportunity exists.

Examples

- RFQ
- Tender
- Budget Inquiry
- Direct Purchase Request

---

## Negotiation

Commercial and technical discussions are underway.

Activities

- Quotations
- Clarifications
- Vendor evaluation
- Technical meetings

---

## Won

Commercial agreement reached.

Outputs

- Sales Order
- Contract
- Project
- Procurement Process

---

## Customer Registration

Customer master record is created.

Mandatory validations

- Legal Information
- Financial Information
- Compliance
- Tax Information

---

## Approval

Internal review completed.

Departments

- Sales
- Finance
- Compliance
- Management

---

## Active Customer

Customer becomes operational across ETA.

Capabilities

- Procurement
- Contracts
- Projects
- Finance
- Support

---

## Strategic Customer

High-value enterprise customer.

Typical Criteria

- High annual revenue
- Long-term cooperation
- Executive relationship
- Multiple active projects

---

## Preferred Customer

Trusted customer receiving operational advantages.

Examples

- Priority quotation
- Dedicated account manager
- Faster response

---

## Inactive

Customer currently has no commercial activity.

Historical data remains available.

---

## Archived

Customer permanently closed.

Rules

- Read-only
- No new transactions
- Historical reporting preserved

---

# Lifecycle Events

Major lifecycle events include

- Lead Created

- Opportunity Created
- RFQ Received
- First Quotation
- First Order
- Contract Signed
- Project Started
- First Payment
- Customer Approved
- Customer Archived

Every event is recorded in the audit history.
