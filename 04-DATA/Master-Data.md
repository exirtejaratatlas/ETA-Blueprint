# ETA Master Data

## Purpose

Master Data represents the core business information that is shared across all enterprise systems.

Every business process within ETA references Master Data instead of creating duplicate records.

ETA follows the Single Source of Truth principle.

---

- Single Source of Truth
- Enterprise Ownership
- Global Uniqueness
- AI Ready
- Version Controlled
