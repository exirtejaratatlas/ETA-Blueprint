Version: 1.0

Status: Living Document

# Purpose

This constitution defines the fundamental principles governing the ETA Ecosystem.

Every strategic, technical, operational and AI decision must comply with this document.

This document overrides temporary decisions, conversations and implementation preferences.

---

# Mission of ETA

ETA exists to become the intelligent operating system of industrial procurement.

Instead of being only a steel trading company, ETA will become a technology-enabled procurement ecosystem connecting customers, suppliers, manufacturers and artificial intelligence into one integrated platform.

---

# Vision

Build the most intelligent procurement ecosystem in the Middle East.

An ecosystem where industrial purchasing becomes:

- Faster
- Smarter
- Transparent
- AI-driven
- Data-driven

---

# Core Philosophy

We are not building:

- a CRM
