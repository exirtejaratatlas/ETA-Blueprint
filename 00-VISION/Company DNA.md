# ETA Company DNA

## Purpose

Company DNA defines the identity of ETA.

It explains who we are, why we exist, how we think, how we make decisions, and what makes ETA fundamentally different from every other procurement software company.

Technology may evolve.

Products may change.

Markets may shift.

Our DNA remains constant.

---

# Who We Are

ETA (Exir Tejarat Atlas) is an Enterprise Procurement Intelligence Company.

We combine industrial expertise, enterprise software, artificial intelligence, and organizational knowledge to build the next generation of intelligent procurement systems.

We are not merely a software company.

We are building the operating system for industrial procurement.

---

# Why We Exist

Industrial procurement is still fragmented.

Critical knowledge is scattered across spreadsheets, emails, documents, and individual experience.

ETA exists to unify people, knowledge, processes, and artificial intelligence into one intelligent ecosystem.

# Our Philosophy

Procurement is not purchasing.

Procurement is strategic decision making.

Every procurement activity generates knowledge.

Knowledge generates intelligence.

Intelligence generates competitive advantage.

ETA exists to preserve and multiply that intelligence.

---

# Enterprise Mindset

We think in systems, not features.

We design platforms, not isolated applications.

We solve business problems, not software problems.

Every capability should strengthen the entire enterprise.
