# Introduction

## Why ETA Exists

The industrial procurement industry is still fragmented, heavily dependent on manual processes, disconnected data, spreadsheets, emails, phone calls, and individual experience.

Organizations often spend significant time and resources searching for reliable suppliers, comparing quotations, validating technical specifications, managing documents, negotiating prices, tracking deliveries, and maintaining knowledge.

ETA (Exir Tejarat Atlas) was conceived to fundamentally transform this process.

Rather than building another ERP or CRM, ETA aims to become an AI-native Enterprise Procurement Intelligence Platform that combines business knowledge, industrial expertise, artificial intelligence, structured data, and automation into a single intelligent ecosystem.

ETA is designed not merely as software, but as the digital brain of industrial procurement.
